Our Mission

Counterfeiting is a $2 trillion global problem that threatens consumer safety, destroys brand value, and funds criminal enterprises. Traditional solutions like holograms and serial numbers are easily replicated.

TAG IT Network combines the immutability of blockchain with the physical security of NFC chips to create unforgeable product identities. Every product gets a unique digital twin that travels with it from manufacturer to consumer.

We believe in a future where consumers can instantly verify the authenticity of any product with a simple tap of their phone, and where the entire supply chain is transparent and trustworthy.

The Problem

  • Holograms and labels easily faked
  • No way to track ownership changes
  • Consumer safety at risk
  • Brands lose billions in revenue

Our Solution

  • Cryptographic NFC authentication
  • Immutable blockchain records
  • Complete ownership history
  • Community-powered verification
