Our Mission

Reshape global trade by turning provenance, compliance, and settlement into verifiable, programmable, low-friction rails.

Enable any authorized party to verify authenticity, ownership, and status - privately when needed, publicly when useful - while enabling safe recovery, recalls, and auditable custody.

TAG IT Network combines the immutability of blockchain with the physical security of NFC chips to create unforgeable product identities. Every product gets a unique digital twin that travels with it from manufacturer to consumer - creating transparent, trustworthy supply chains.
