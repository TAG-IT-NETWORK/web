üîÑ Product Life Cycle

Seven Stages of Verified Trust

Every product goes through a complete lifecycle, each stage recorded immutably on-chain.

01

Mint

Manufacturer

Create a digital twin NFT on TAG IT L2 blockchain. This unique token represents the physical product throughout its entire lifecycle.

TAGITCore.mintTwin() Product metadata, batch info, origin
02

Bind

Manufacturer

Link the physical NFC chip to the digital twin through cryptographic binding. This creates an unbreakable connection between physical and digital.

TAGITCore.bindChip() Chip UID, tamper detection hash
03

Activate

Retailer

Activate the product for sale with location and timestamp data. This marks the product as ready for consumer purchase.

TAGITCore.activate() Location, retailer ID, timestamp
04

Claim

Consumer

Consumer takes ownership via NFC scan. Ownership is transferred on-chain, activating warranty and proving authenticity.

TAGITCore.claim() Ownership transfer, warranty activation
05

Flag

Consumer/Authority

Report suspicious products with evidence. This initiates the arbitration workflow for investigation.

TAGITRecovery.flag() Evidence hash, reporter ID
06

Resolve

Arbitrator

DAO-elected arbitrators investigate and resolve disputes through multi-sig process. Outcomes: verify authentic, confirm counterfeit, or inconclusive.

TAGITRecovery.resolve() Multi-sig, verdict, evidence
07

Recycle

Certified Recycler

End-of-life processing by certified recyclers. The digital twin is burned, creating a permanent disposal record.

TAGITCore.recycle() Disposal verification, compliance
