‚ú¶ Core Features

Why TAG IT Network?

The complete infrastructure for blockchain-powered product authentication.

üîê

Cryptographic Security

Every product gets a unique cryptographic identity that's impossible to forge or duplicate.

‚ñº

How It Works

Each physical product is paired with an NFC chip containing a unique cryptographic key. When scanned, the chip signs a challenge from our network, proving authenticity without ever exposing the private key.

Technical Specs

  • ECDSA signatures (secp256k1)
  • Hardware-isolated private keys
  • 256-bit entropy per chip
  • Tamper-evident: chip destruction = detectable on-chain
‚ö°

Instant Verification

Authenticate any product in under 0.3 seconds with a simple NFC tap.

‚ñº

How It Works

Our optimized verification flow uses edge caching and pre-computed merkle proofs to deliver instant results. The NFC chip responds to the challenge, and our network validates against the on-chain state in milliseconds.

Technical Specs

  • Average verification time: 280ms
  • 99.99% uptime SLA
  • Global edge network (50+ PoPs)
  • Offline verification support via cached proofs
üîó

Full Traceability

Track the complete journey from manufacturer to consumer with immutable records.

‚ñº

How It Works

Every lifecycle event‚Äîfrom minting to claiming‚Äîis recorded on-chain with timestamps, locations, and actor signatures. This creates an immutable audit trail that can be verified by anyone, anytime.

Technical Specs

  • EigenDA data availability layer
  • Permanent storage on Ethereum L1
  • GPS + timestamp attestations
  • Full event indexing via subgraph

‚ñº

How It Works

Our AI engine analyzes patterns across millions of verification events to detect counterfeiting attempts, predict supply chain disruptions, and optimize inventory management in real-time.

Technical Specs

  • Anomaly detection (99.7% accuracy)
  • Predictive analytics (7-day forecast)
  • Real-time fraud alerts (<1s latency)
  • Custom ML models per industry vertical

Cross-Chain Compatible

‚ñº

How It Works

Chainlink CCIP enables secure cross-chain messaging, allowing digital twins to be verified and transferred across Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and other major networks without bridging risks.

Technical Specs

  • Supported: Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base
  • Native bridge (no third-party risk)
